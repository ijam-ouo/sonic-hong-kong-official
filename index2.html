Sonic Hong Kong:

Sonic Exploration of Hong Kong

The project is a collective effort to explore layers of history, hidden stories, and living experiences of Hong Kong through sonic expressions.
The themes include Hidden Stories, Sunday Voices, Sonic Youth, Sound Bites.
